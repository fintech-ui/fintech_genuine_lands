Our Story

Built in Owerri. Trusted across Nigeria.

FinTech Genuine Properties began as a small documentation and land-verification desk in Owerri, Imo State, formed in response to how often buyers were losing money to unverified land and forged titles. Twelve years on, that same commitment to verified paperwork now backs a nationwide portfolio spanning residential, commercial and investment property in all 36 states.

Every listing on this platform passes through our internal verification process before it ever reaches a client — because in Nigerian real estate, trust is the only currency that matters.

Mission

To make verified, well-documented property ownership accessible to every Nigerian, wherever they live.

Vision

To be Nigeria's most trusted name in property investment — one verified transaction at a time.
